ChickenEaters

Vital information about chicken for eaters of chicken

I will be judging each chicken shop from which I purchase delicious chicken against the benchmark of multinational chicken purveyor KFC. I will be measuring the quality of each based on the following criteria

  • 2 Pieces of Chicken
  • Wings of any number(not BBQ)
  • Fries
  • Sauces - BBQ and Ketchup mainly, with occassional special mentions. Mayo will not be considered as it is a monstrosity, a crime against food etc, etc (unless it is garlic mayo, in which case all is forgiven)
  • Drinks - one can tell a great deal about a person (chicken shop) by the selection of beveages on offer.
